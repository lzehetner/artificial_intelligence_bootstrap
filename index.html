Introduction

Artifical Intelligence or AI is the simulation of human thought and behaviour by computers and machine systems. It is used to solve complex tasks normally done by humans, such as pattern and trend recognition, decision making and translation. In recent years, AI technology has developed rapidly and is being used in more and more fields, such as medicine, finance and even the entertainment industry. However, it is also a controversial topic as it raises questions regarding ethics and jobs

Operation Of AI

  • Monitoring: An artificial intelligence can process and analyze large amounts of data and detect deviation in a short time. This capability is great for monitoring applications, detecting environmental changes, cyber security, etc.
  • Detection: AI can intelligently develop solutions from large data sets using dynamic models (data mining) and recognize abstract patterns as well as gain insights from them
  • Predict and Interpret: The system can make predictions and build models to predict future trends. It can also recognize patterns from images or video files and interpret them. This type of analysis offers much more freedom than traditional data analysis methods

Machine Learning

Machine Learning is a sub-field of artificial intelligence that enables systems to learn and improve automatically from experience (data) without being explicitly programmed

Machine learning can learn the following tasks
  • Value prediction make decisions on the basis of the analysed data (e.g. electricity consumption or sales forecast)

  • Calculation of probabilities for certain events (bps. purchase probability or termination probability)

  • Recognise groups and clustering in one data set

  • Recognition of interrelationships in sequences

  • Reduction of dimensions without much loss of information

  • Optimization of business processes

Deep Learning

Deep Learning is a special method for processing information and a sub-area of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. Deep Learning uses neural networks to analyze large data sets. This method of operation used by Deep Learning acts in a similar way to the human brain. Data is first extracted and then analyzed to generate a conclusion or forecast. In practice, deep learning is mainly used to recognize images, understand texts or make better decisions.
